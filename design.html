One4All Gift Card Design & Practice

Project Description

During this project we were given the task of creating a gift card for the One4All competition with a theme of happiness. My idea was to create a pot of gold under a rainbow as that is quite a common symbol of happiness but also luck.

Project Details

  • Created for the One4All annual competition.
  • Made in Adobe Illustrator.
  • Gift card design along with a carrier.
